Group Process

Assignment 2 was a success, although we lost park in only one part which has taught us valuable lessons as we move on to Assignment 3. In Assignment 2, we divided the work equally and worked in them independently. Now, in Assignment 3 we realised how important working as a group and discussing topics and tasks as a group is. So, now before working individually on each of our assigned tasks, we discuss what the task is asking first and the information that it requires and what we expect as a group to see written on those parts. This change in our process has proved to be really helpful as we worked through finishing assignment 3.
